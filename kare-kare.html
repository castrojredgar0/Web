Kare-Kare Recipe
Ingredients
1 kg oxtail or beef, cut into serving pieces 1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup annatto seeds soaked in 1/4 cup water 2 tbsp bagoong (fermented shrimp paste)
1 banana heart, sliced 1 bundle string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
2 eggplants, sliced 1 bunch pechay (bok choy)
1 onion, chopped 4 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and pepper to taste 2 tbsp oil
Instructions
1. Boil the oxtail or beef in water until tender. Set aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant.
3. Add the annatto water, peanut butter, and bagoong. Stir well.
4. Add the boiled meat and continue to cook for a few minutes.
5. Add the vegetables (banana heart, string beans, eggplants, pechay) and simmer until cooked.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice and extra bagoong on the side. Enjoy!
