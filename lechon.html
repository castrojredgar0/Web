Lechon Recipe
Ingredients
1 whole pig, cleaned and de-haired 10 stalks of lemongrass, bruised and tied in knots
1 cup soy sauce 1 cup vinegar
2 heads of garlic, minced 1 cup salt
1 cup black peppercorns 4 liters of water
Banana leaves
Instructions
1. Clean the pig thoroughly, removing any remaining hair.
2. Rub the inside of the pig with salt and peppercorns, ensuring even distribution.
3. Stuff the pig's cavity with lemongrass, garlic, and banana leaves.
4. Sew the pig's belly closed using a kitchen twine or heavy-duty thread.
5. Skewer the pig on a large bamboo pole or metal spit.
6. Prepare the basting mixture by combining soy sauce, vinegar, and water in a large bowl.
7. Roast the pig over an open fire or charcoal pit, basting frequently with the mixture until the skin is crispy and the meat is cooked through.
8. Once done, carefully remove the pig from the spit and let it rest before carving.
9. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy!
