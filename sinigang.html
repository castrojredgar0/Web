Sinigang Recipe
Ingredients
1 kg pork belly or ribs, cut into serving pieces 1 pack sinigang mix or 1 cup fresh tamarind juice
1 bunch kangkong (water spinach) 1 bunch sitaw (string beans)
2 tomatoes, quartered 1 radish, sliced
1 eggplant, sliced 1 onion, chopped
3 cups water 2 tbsp fish sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
1. In a pot, boil the pork in water until tender. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
2. Add the tomatoes and onion. Continue to simmer until the vegetables are soft.
3. Add the sinigang mix or fresh tamarind juice. Stir well.
4. Add the radish, eggplant, sitaw, and kangkong. Simmer until the vegetables are cooked.
5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
