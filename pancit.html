Pancit Recipe
Ingredients
1 pack Pancit Canton noodles 200g pork, sliced thinly
1 cup shrimp, peeled and deveined 1 cup cabbage, sliced
1 carrot, julienned 1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced 1 bell pepper, sliced
2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp fish sauce
2 cups chicken broth Salt and pepper to taste
2 tbsp oil
Instructions
1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
3. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
4. Add soy sauce and fish sauce, then stir well.
5. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
6. Add vegetables (cabbage, carrot, bell pepper) and cook until tender.
7. Add Pancit Canton noodles and cook until the noodles absorb the broth.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!
