Halo-Halo Recipe
Ingredients
1 cup crushed ice 1/2 cup sweetened beans
1/2 cup sweetened bananas 1/2 cup nata de coco
1/2 cup sweetened jackfruit 1/2 cup sweetened palm fruit
1/2 cup sweetened purple yam (ube) 1/2 cup coconut strips
1/2 cup leche flan 1 scoop of ice cream (optional)
Evaporated milk to taste
Instructions
1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened beans, bananas, nata de coco, jackfruit, palm fruit, purple yam, and coconut strips.
2. Add a layer of crushed ice on top of the layered ingredients.
3. Pour evaporated milk over the crushed ice to your desired amount.
4. Top with leche flan and a scoop of ice cream if desired.
5. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Halo-Halo!
