Adobo Recipe
Ingredients
1 kg chicken or pork, cut into serving pieces 1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup vinegar 1 head garlic, minced
2 bay leaves 1 tsp peppercorns
1 cup water 2 tbsp oil
Salt to taste
Instructions
1. Marinate the meat in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic for at least 30 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a pan and saute the garlic until golden brown.
3. Add the marinated meat and cook until browned.
4. Pour in the marinade along with water, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until meat is tender and the sauce has thickened.
6. Season with salt to taste. Serve with rice and enjoy!
